In this "data" folder there are different versions of the BlackFriday csv's:

BlackFriday_test & BlackFriday_train are the original csvs.

BlackFriday_test_ready & BlackFriday_train_ready are csvs with baseline data cleaning/engineering.

BlackFriday_test_ready1 & BlackFriday_train_ready1 are csvs with version 1 data cleaning/engineering.

BlackFriday_test_ready3 & BlackFriday_train_ready3 are csvs with version 3 data cleaning/engineering.

Note: version 1 resulted in training the best predictive model


black_friday_test_w_prediction contains the final predictions for purchases based on the final model
